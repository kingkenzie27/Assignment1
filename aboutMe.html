Hello, I'm Makenzie
Hello, My name is Makenzie King. I am a sophomore at Saginaw Valley State University majoring in Computer Science. I am also a clarinet player in the marching band and attempting to start an RSO for FIRST Robotics students. My hometown is Lapeer, Michigan.
I have been programming since 8th grade through a code.org class. Afterward, I joined my high school's competitive robotics team. After spending four years on the team, I fell in love with programming autonomous robotic actions and chose to grow my knowledge in college.
